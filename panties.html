Saturday, February 16, 2019 at 8 PM

Get your tickets now!!!!

The biggest show in Hampton Roads is back!!!

Panties in a Twist is a sketch comedy show written by women ... directed by women ... and starring women. The Push Comedy Theater has gathered Hampton Roads' finest and funniest female writers, actresses and comedians for the countries largest, all-woman, comedy show. With all these funny women in one place, this is guaranteed to be a huge event. Panties in a Twist is one of Hampton Roads' most talked about shows ... so make sure your'e part of history and get your tickets now!
